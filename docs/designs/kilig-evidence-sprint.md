## Vision

### 10x Check
The 10x version of the sprint is not a bigger sprint — it is the same sprint (≤$700 ad-spend, ~$800 all-in with brand reservations) that also seeds every future asset: the brand surface reserved before the name goes public, every interview quote captured as reusable fuel, every creative pre-screened before spend, and the diaspora hypothesis queued as a fast-follow. Nothing built in the sprint is throwaway; the opt-in list, the creator relationships, and the validated story hooks all carry into the studio phase (design doc Phase 2) and eventually the app (Phase 3).

## Accepted Scope (added to this plan)
- AI-generated ad creatives via Higgsfield: **≥2 interactive-hook and ≥2 passive-teaser variants** (4–6 total), synthetic-media disclosure labeled, one generation pass per variant plus at most one quality re-roll each (bounds the effort at roughly half a day, not 30 minutes — the design doc's own quality-bar caution applies). Per cross-model review D12: **all four variants go live (2 per category, even budget split)**; the virality predictor is a pre-launch sanity ranking only. The kill/continue gate compares category-average CTRs (≥ 1.5×) with absolute floors (interactive CTR ≥ 1%, cost per net follow ≤ $1).
- Day-0 checklist gains: reserve @kilig (or nearest variants) on TikTok/FB/IG and register kilig.ph / kilig.app before any public ad runs (~$50–100/yr, outside the ad budget — total sprint cost ceiling becomes ~$800: $700 ad-spend worst case + reservations).
- Execution approach for the sprint: **instrumented kit** (decision row 7) — landing page with UTM-split tracking + opt-in capture, interview script/kit, ad brief with thresholds baked in, tracking spreadsheet. CC-built, ~1 hour.
